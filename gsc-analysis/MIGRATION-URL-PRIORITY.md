## Notes for migration
- Homepage alone ~27.7k clicks (~59% of listed page clicks).
- Slash variants matter: e.g. blog modem Huawei with and without trailing slash both rank — preserve or 301 one to the other consistently.
- Affiliate paths `/2ghasemi` and `/shahin` have real SEO value.
- `/app/profiles` is app surface — reverse-proxy or keep off WordPress content.
- Hash URLs (`#blog1`) are Search Console fragments — not separate pages to migrate.
- Keep `www` → `https://itanet.ir/` redirect (www still has impressions).
- Traffic is ~87% mobile — WP theme/Elementor must be mobile-first.
